Feature

Up-to-date Jobs Listing

The mobile application will automatically update daily new jobs. Just browse the job listings and click to read more information. Once synchronised, you can read about the jobs offline, no need for an internet connection.

Notifications

Get automated notifications on the application as well as in your email for any jobs that you are interested in. All you need to do is tell the application your desired job titles, for example “Drilling Supervisor”, “Company Man”, “Drill Site Manager”.

Favourite Jobs

If you see some jobs that you like, simply make them a favourite and choose to send the job information to your email. From there, you can easily open the link and apply directly to those jobs. Be quick and get that job first!
